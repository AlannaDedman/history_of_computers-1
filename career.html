SAGE Project

From 1961 to 1963, she worked on the Semi-Automatic Ground Environment Project at the MIT Lab. Which was an extension of Project Whirlwind. The SAGE Project was developed for the military to locate enemy aircrafts.

Source: Margaret Hamiltion In Action, Wikipedia, Margaret Hamiltion (software engineer)

MIT Instrumentation Laboratory and the Apollo Guidance Computer

In 1965, she became aware of the of the Apollo project and wanted to join it. She became the first programmer hired and was the first female programmer for this project at the MIT Insrumentation Lab. She was responsible for tesing the software for the Apollo spacecraft's Command and Lunar Module.

Margaret Hamiltion, Wikipedia, Margaret Hamiltion (software engineer)

Apollo 11 "Moon Landing"

This event marked the first time humans landed on the moon. Hamiltion was nominated for the NASA Space Act Award for her work.
