Early Life

Source: Students in Decatur Homesteads, Legends of America, (legendsofamerica.com)

Margaret was born on August 17th, 1936, in Paoli, Indiana. She was born to Kenneth Heafield and Ruth Esther Heafield. Her family lived in Indiana for a short time before moving to Michigan, where Margaret went to school. Margaret has a younger sister named Kathryn Heafield.

They were raised and grew up in Michigan's Upper Peninsula.

Source: Upper Peninsula Map, Wikipedia, (wikipedia.com)


She graduated from Hancock High School in 1954. Her father was said to be a poet, something that inspired her to minor in philosphy in university. She married James Cox Hamiltion on June 15, 1958. They had a daughter named Lauren Heafield on November 10, 1959. After divorcing her husband in 1967, she married Dan Lickly two years later.
